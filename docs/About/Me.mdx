### ~~weeb~~ Japanese Media

It's hard to tell what specifically draws me to Japanese writing. I really enjoy the way some anime are directed that I don't really see in Western style animation. The creativity oozing from some of the best works in anime is crazy.

Examples of creative use of animation:
- [This entire Mob Psycho sequence](https://youtu.be/b5v9IYYk968?si=fNytRWSv9nltFebc)
- Bocchi The Rock has many, *many* examples
- Monogatari with its use of character lensing and funky, borderline nonsensical scenes to keep the audience engaged with what are essentially entire seasons of mostly people talking

Some of the 'weirder' styles in Japanese writing strike a chord with me in a way no Western writing ever has.

Examples: 

- Nisio Isin (the monogatari series) 
- Nasu (Fate Stay Night, the rest of the Nasuverse) writes a lot about abstract concepts
