# What is this site?

A place for me to share various notes I wish someone else had written for me when I needed them. Also, random ramblings I might have.

## Why?

~~I have an unhealthy obsession with documenting shit.~~

### Helping people

Helping people feels good. If I have helped at least one person or made someone's day more interesting, the effort was worth it.

### AI
The Internet has been filled with bots for a long time, but I remember a time when interactions felt more organic. 

In an AI-slop infested world where the discerning reader has to constantly second guess every other sentence, I promise any poorly written content on this site is poorly written by a human. Same goes for the code.

### Writing

I realize that I am not great at writing and expressing my thoughts. I hope with practice I will get better.
